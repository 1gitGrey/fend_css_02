Black Panther

Close examination of a Black Panther reveals that there is no difference between this species and its Jaguar "cousin."

  • Scientific Name: Pantera pardus
  • Maximum Speed: 35 mph
  • Average Lifespan: 12-15 years
  • Habitat: Dense rain forests

Identified by its melanism, cougars, leopards, and jaguars alike are better known as panthers to a layperson. Dominantly accessed in jaguars (reessively acquired in cougars and leopards), this fiersome predator would be spotted were it not for a mutation in its gene sequence allowing for it to hunt more effectively in shadows.
